## About Me

I am an AI Software Engineer who builds systems where machine learning models meet production software. My work spans computer vision, natural language processing, and full stack engineering, with a consistent focus on taking a model from a research notebook to something that runs reliably at scale.

At Avancod I have shipped AI financial assistants, NLP pipelines, and NeuroMark, a facial recognition attendance system currently processing over ten thousand records daily at ninety eight point seven percent accuracy. I approach engineering with a product mindset, caring as much about interpretability, security, and user experience as I do about model performance.

**Open To**

- Full stack engineering positions involving machine learning
- Graduate research opportunities in autonomous systems and applied AI
- Collaboration on open source AI tooling

| Impact | Demonstrates applied time series forecasting for financial decision support |

AlgoDinero explores how classical statistical models and modern deep learning architectures compare when forecasting financial time series, with an emphasis on rigorous evaluation.
